Powering Uganda's Food Processing Sector

The agri-food industry is the backbone of Uganda's economy. Ogies provides the equipment, expertise, and ongoing support to help food processors operate more efficiently, reduce waste, and meet both local and export quality standards.

From processing lines to packaging and quality control, we are your one-stop partner for agri-food industrial solutions in East Africa.

Our Agri-food Solutions

Comprehensive equipment and services for every stage of food processing and production.

Processing Lines

Complete automated and semi-automated production lines for fruits, vegetables, grains, and dairy-based products.

  • Washing & sorting systems
  • Blanching & cooking units
  • Mixing & blending equipment
  • Filling & portioning machines

Packaging Solutions

Modern packaging systems that extend shelf life, maintain hygiene, and improve product presentation for retail markets.

  • Vacuum packaging machines
  • Modified atmosphere packaging (MAP)
  • Pouch & sachet filling
  • Labelling & coding systems

Quality Control Systems

Equipment and instrumentation to ensure consistent product quality and compliance with food safety regulations.

  • Metal detection systems
  • Weight & checkweighing
  • Temperature monitoring
  • Microbiological testing kits

Cold Chain & Storage

Refrigeration and cold storage solutions to reduce post-harvest losses and maintain product integrity throughout the supply chain.

  • Walk-in cold rooms
  • Blast chillers & freezers
  • Refrigerated transport
  • Temperature logging systems

Grain & Milling Equipment

Efficient milling and grain processing solutions supporting Uganda's maize, sorghum, and millet industries.

  • Hammer mills & roller mills
  • Grain cleaners & graders
  • Silos & storage systems
  • Flour packaging units

Consulting & Training

Expert consulting to help you select the right equipment, design efficient production layouts, and train your staff.

  • Plant layout & design
  • HACCP implementation support
  • Operator training programs
  • Regulatory compliance guidance
