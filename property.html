Our Property Services

Full-spectrum property management services for agricultural land, residential estates, and commercial facilities.

Land Management

Expert management of agricultural land, ensuring proper utilization, boundary protection, and compliance with Uganda's land regulations.

  • Land title verification & registration
  • Boundary surveys & demarcation
  • Land use planning
  • Dispute resolution support

Facility Maintenance

Proactive and reactive maintenance services to keep your properties in excellent condition and protect your investment value.

  • Scheduled preventive maintenance
  • Emergency repairs
  • Contractor coordination
  • Property inspection reports

Tenant Relations

Professional management of all tenant-related matters, ensuring smooth relationships and timely rent collection.

  • Tenant screening & vetting
  • Lease preparation & management
  • Rent collection & disbursement
  • Conflict mediation

Financial Reporting

Transparent, detailed financial reports so you always know how your property is performing.

  • Monthly income statements
  • Expense tracking
  • Rental yield analysis
  • Tax compliance documentation

Property Valuation

Accurate property valuations to support buying, selling, or leasing decisions based on current Kampala market data.

  • Market appraisals
  • Investment feasibility studies
  • Comparative market analysis
  • Rental pricing guidance

Legal & Compliance

Ensuring your property operations comply with all Ugandan property laws, local authority regulations, and environmental requirements.

  • Permits & licensing management
  • Lease compliance oversight
  • Eviction procedures (legal)
  • Environmental compliance
