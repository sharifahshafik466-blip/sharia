Our Story

Founded in Kampala, Ogies Dairy & Property Management has grown over a decade into one of Uganda's most reliable providers of dairy processing equipment and agricultural property solutions.

We began with a simple vision: to help Ugandan dairy farmers access modern, efficient processing technology that was previously out of reach. Today, we serve over 100 clients across the country, from small-scale farmers to industrial processors.

Our property management division grew organically as clients sought trusted partners for managing their agricultural and commercial assets across the Kampala region.
